Malls

I12 (Katong Mall)

Address:
112 E Coast Rd, Singapore 428802

112 Katong, formerly Katong Mall and Katong People's Complex, is a seven-storey shopping mall in the east of Singapore located at the intersection of East Coast Road and Joo Chiat Road. The mall opened in November 2011.

Completed in late 1983 as Katong People's Complex, it had an exterior facade resembling a prison cell, such as having exposed blue pipes. It was a strata titled mall back then, comprising a department store and various shops and eateries. There are also numerous medical clinics inside the mall.

In 1995, the facade was revamped and the mall was renamed Katong Mall. The department store was replaced by Cold Storage supermarket, and various education centres occupied the upper floors of the mall. The mall's basements contained an electronics store and various strata titled units.

In 2009, because of bad business, Pua Seck Guan, former chief executive of CapitaLand Retail, bought Katong Mall in a $247.55 million deal as part of an en bloc sale. A private trust called Perennial Katong Retail Trust was subsequently formed to buy the mall from Tuan Sing Holdings' Golden Cape Investment. The trust's investors included corporate and institutional investors and Pua.

The mall closed its doors in mid 2010 for a major makeover, to help revitalize the mall. When it reopened as 112 Katong in late 2011, it featured a Peranakan theme, as a reminder of its location. The mall also featured a cinema, a food court, a gourmet supermarket, banks and more than 150 specialty shops. It also featured a wet and dry playground on the fourth floor.

Katong Shopping Centre

Address:
865 Mountbatten Rd, Singapore 437844

Katong Shopping Centre is a shopping mall located along Mountbatten Road in Singapore. Established in 1971 and opened to the public in 1973, it is the first air-conditioned mall in Singapore. Katong Shopping Centre is situated around Odeon Theater, Church of the Holy Family, and Roxy Square, as well as several hotels, such as Paramount Hotel and the Grand Mercure Roxy Hotel. The aging mall was once home to mostly textile, shoe and food outlets, and now houses a large number of services which include tailoring, photocopying/printing and several maid employment agencies.

Roxy Square

50 East Coast Road, Singapore 428769

Roxy Square Shopping Centre is primarily used for Commercial & Residential rental and sale. It has a variety of services, from tuition to furniture. There is also a small food court on the first floor. I love the laksa there. The stall is called Janggut Laksa.

Katong V

Address:
30 E Coast Rd, Singapore 428751

Katong V is strategically located at the heart of Katong, an established middle to high income enclave, a mature estate with a large number of landed properties and condominiums and is also located close to a mix of popular schools, residential estates and hotels.

Katong V, a mall inspired by Katong's Peranakan heritage is set to capture your attention. You will discover a vibrant blend of F&B concepts, lifestyle stores, retail shops, educational centres and services, including a supermarket.

Katong Square

Address:
86-88 E Coast Rd, Singapore 428788

Built on the site of the former Joo Chiat Police Station, Katong Square is situated in the heart of Katong and boasts the largest open public space in the vicinity, creating a unique neighbourhood gathering point. It mainly houses many restaurants.
