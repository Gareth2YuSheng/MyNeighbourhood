Joo Chiat Community Club

I see this place everyday on my way home. Usually there are many activities you can sign up for here, such as dance or floral arrangement. Some other events are held here as well, e.g. the Edusave awards, which is held in a big hall. I have been inside this place a few times when I was younger, this place is 3 or 4 stories high and has a small ‘garden’ in the third floor.
